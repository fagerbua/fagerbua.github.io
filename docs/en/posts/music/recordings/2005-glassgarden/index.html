    Recording from the Glass Hall of 'Dronning Mauds minne' college, Trondheim, Norway, 2005

    An ad-hoc recording while trying out this organ at the former Dalen School for the Blind, where my father was a pupil in the early 1970s. It probably sounds quite a bit in it’s current glass building than it did in the gym hall where it used to stand. (I’m told taking pipes out of the organ was a popular pasttime among the pupils back then.)

    Louis Vierne: Intermezzo from Fantasy Pieces op. 51

    Thanks to Espen Selbæk, Nils Petter Svendsen Aune, and Jon Kristian Fjellestad for showing me this instrument and more in the Trondheim region.
